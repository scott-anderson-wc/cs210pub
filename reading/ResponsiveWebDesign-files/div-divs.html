The following version of the CS110 schedule has two different looks, depending on the screen width:

  • wide (> 768px): it looks like a table with each week taking up one row. Lines are in blue;
  • narrow (≤ 768px): it looks like stacked blocks with each week being a block and each day being a block within the box. Lines around the outer blocks are in green.

The markup (HTML structure) is identical: divs nested inside divs.
