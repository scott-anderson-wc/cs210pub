Navigational Structures:

Linear and Circular

  • Follows pre-defined path

Example: Slide Show with next and previous buttons.

Spoke and Hub

Example: Club Philipina in the CS110 Hall of Fame.

Clique

  • Every page on site linked to every other page.

Rooted Tree

  • Intuitive navigation

Examples:

  • CS110 site, assignments section

Network (Graph)

  • A page might be conected to a number of other pages (random interconnection, based on logical content of pages on site)

 

Example: Wellesley College Web Site

 

In most of the web sites, with more than just a few pages, you will see a combination of several of the above structures.
